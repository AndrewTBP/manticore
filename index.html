Manticore

13th Age fair encounter generator

Manticore implements the rules for Building Battles on page 186 of your 13th Age core rulebook, in particular the pricing table. This application will automatically produce a set of all – within reason – encounter combinations that are allowed for a given party and selection of monsters.

To produce an unfair encounter, refer to the guidelines on page 187 of your rulebook to modify a fair encounter generated by this app.
